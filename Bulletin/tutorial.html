How to create a new memo?

1. Click "Create New Memo" on top-left side.

2. Enter title and context of memo.

3. Drag the memo to desired position.

How to delete a memo?

1. Double Click on the memo.

2. Comfirm the deletion.



How to create a new picture?

1. Click "Create New Picture" on top-left side.

2. Enter title and context of picture.

3. Drag the picture to desired position.

How to delete a picture?

1. Double Click on the picture.

2. Comfirm the deletion.
